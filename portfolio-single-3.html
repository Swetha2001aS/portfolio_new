Project Description

Cardio Vista, developed during my extended KELTRON internship,is a heart health analysis app utilizing sound technology and machine learning. The project involved collecting and processing raw data to enhance diagnostic accuracy. An extension of the internship further refined these technologies, integrating advanced models for improved real-time analysis while ensuring regulatory compliance.
